About Us

At CodeSafari, we believe learning to code should feel like exploring a jungle full of hidden treasures. Instead of dull lessons, we guide kids through exciting adventures where every tag, every line of code, is like discovering a new animal or plant in the wild. Our mission is simple: make HTML learning playful, creative, and unforgettable for curious young explorers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to take children on an adventurous journey into the world of coding. With interactive lessons, colorful challenges, and a supportive team of guides, we transform learning HTML into a safari filled with curiosity, courage, and creativity.
